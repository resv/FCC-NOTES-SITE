About this site

Hand writing notes can take too long, and a laptop can be inefficient if you're trying to learn and write notes simultaneously. This site was designed to free up users of writing notes giving them one less burden while learning on FreeCodeCamp . This can also be advantageous for senior users who are well toward the backend section of FreeCodeCamp's map, allowing quick and easily accessible notes for easy referencing. Learn without the worry of note taking, so you can focus on the problems.
